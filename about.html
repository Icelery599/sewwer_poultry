About Sewwer Poultry

Our story, mission, and commitment to ethical farming.

Our Story

Sewwer Poultry was established in 2025 with a clear mission: to provide healthy, high-quality Noiler birds, fresh eggs, poultry manure, and mature birds for consumption while maintaining ethical and sustainable farming practices.

Located at TY Danjuma Way, ATC, Kofai, Nigeria, our farm combines modern biosecurity measures with traditional care to raise birds that are strong, disease-free, and naturally fed. We believe in transparency, quality, and customer satisfaction above everything else.

Our Mission

To produce and deliver premium poultry products that improve nutrition and livelihoods, while upholding the highest standards of animal welfare.

Our Vision

To become a leading poultry farm in Nigeria known for integrity, innovation, and a commitment to healthy, organic farming.

Our Values

Ethics, quality, transparency, and customer-centric service. Every bird matters, and every customer deserves the best.
