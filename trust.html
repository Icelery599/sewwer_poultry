Trust & Quality Assurance

How we ensure every product meets the highest health and ethical standards.

Biosecurity Measures

Our farm operates strict biosecurity protocols: sanitized coops, restricted visitor access, footbaths, and regular disinfection. We maintain a closed flock to prevent disease introduction.

Vaccination Program

All birds follow a comprehensive vaccination schedule from day-old chicks to maturity. This protects against Newcastle disease, Gumboro, and other common poultry illnesses.

Ethical Farming

We provide spacious, clean housing, natural feeds, fresh water, and free-range access. No growth hormones or antibiotics are used routinely; birds are treated humanely at every stage.

Commitment to Health

Our vaccination chart is reviewed by veterinary professionals. We also conduct regular health checks and maintain records for full traceability. When you buy from Sewwer Poultry, you're buying safe, healthy birds and products.

We also test our organic manure to ensure it's free from harmful pathogens, making it safe for your garden or farm.
